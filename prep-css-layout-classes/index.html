Indiana Jones

Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Lost Ark

The first Indiana Jones movie introduced us to the archeology adventurer Indiana Jones. The movie follows his attempts to stop nazi villains from using the ark of the covenant to take over the world.

Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom

The second movie of the trilogy, the Temple of Doom follows Indiana on an adventure to stop an evil cult from enslaving children of the surrounding villages.

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

The third movie, and certainly the best of all, the Last Crusade follows Indiana as he is re-united with his father and they adventure to find the Holy Grail and once again stop Nazi's from using for evil means
